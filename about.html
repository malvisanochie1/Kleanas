Our Story

A Vision for Cleaner, Healthier Spaces

Kleanas was founded with a simple mission: to provide top-quality cleaning services to homes and businesses across Nigeria. Our journey began when our founder, John Doe, saw a gap in the market for reliable and professional cleaning services that prioritize both quality and customer satisfaction. Inspired by the need for cleaner, healthier living and working environments, John Doe established Kleanas to bring a new level of excellence to the cleaning industry.
